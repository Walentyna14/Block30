Wikipedia about Pietrzwałd

  • Pietrzwałd [ˈpjɛtʂfau̯t] (German: Peterswalde) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sztum, within Sztum County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) east of Sztum and 57 km (35 mi) south-east of the regional capital Gdańsk.
  • Before 1772 the area was part of Kingdom of Poland, 1772-1945 Prussia and Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
  • The village has a population of 180.
